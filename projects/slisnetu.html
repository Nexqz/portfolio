Slisnetu

Objective:

Slisnetu is a versatile utensil box and survival kit designed to enhance your chances of thriving in the wild or during an apocalypse. Crafted from durable stainless steel, this compact container includes two forks, two spoons, and two pairs of chopsticks, all ready for use in both culinary delights and survival situations. Every element of the kit is thoughtfully designed for maximum functionality, ensuring that each piece serves a distinct purpose. The sliding box cleverly transforms into two cups, while the divider doubles as a sharp blade for cutting and self-defense. Tailored for adventurers of all ages, Slisnetu is your essential companion for surviving the great outdoors. Slisnetu; eat, drink and survive together.

Tools:

Adobe Suite, Figma
